Features

Everything you need
to stay ahead

LENS reads the chain so you don't have to. Open any profile on X and the full picture loads right under their name, no tabs, no explorers, no guessing.

01
Dev Claim Fee

Every time a dev pulls fees from their contract on Base, you see it. If the money walks straight to an exchange, that's the clearest sell signal on-chain.

BASE CHAIN
02
Deploy Count

How many contracts has this wallet shipped before? Serial deployers light up automatically, with their full deploy history one glance away.

ON-CHAIN
03
PleaseBro Detection

Catch when someone quietly earns fees from a token another dev deployed. LENS matches deployer against fee recipient straight from Bankrbot, so there's no guesswork.

BANKRBOT
04
CA Hunter

Digs through bio, pinned post, recent tweets and links for any contract address, then resolves the token, deployer and fee structure behind it.

BIO INTEL
05
GitHub Intel

When a dev links their GitHub, you see repos, languages and last commit, cross-referenced against the contracts they've shipped on-chain.

Coming Soon
06
Username History

Every handle they've ever worn on X, kept on record. A scammer can rebrand all they want, you'll still know exactly who they are.

Coming Soon
07
Bundled Wallets

Traces who funded the early buyers and groups the wallets bundled behind a single dev into one cluster. You see the real crew running the play, not just the face out front.

08
Smart Followers

Shows which high-signal on-chain accounts and KOLs actually follow a profile, so you can tell real traction from bought noise in seconds.
